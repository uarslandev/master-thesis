> Design the UI to match the natural "information flow" of a clinician, respecting how they mentally process data and move through temporally ordered diagnostic steps.

"Feedback loops where acquisition of new data improves certainty and generates new questions to examine,"

> <b>Support Iterative Discovery.</b> The UI should be designed for "feedback loops," allowing clinicians to add new data or observations and see how the AI's certainty levels change in real-time.
